Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding safe gutter clearing, clog prevention, and exterior brightening.

How often should I have my gutters cleaned?

We recommend cleaning gutters at least twice a year: once in late spring to clear maple seeds and pollen, and once in late fall after all leaves have dropped to prepare for winter ice.

Do you clean gutters with gutter guards installed?

Yes. We can remove or lift guards to clear out fine silt and organic debris that builds up underneath, clean the guards themselves, and re-secure them.

What is gutter brightening or tiger striping removal?

Black streaks on gutter exteriors are caused by electrostatic bonding of acid rain residue and roofing runoff. Standard washing won't remove them. We use specialized cleaning agents to break this bond and restore the white color.
