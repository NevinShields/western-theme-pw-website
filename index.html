Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here is what homeowners and estate managers ask us most about our soft-wash and pressure cleaning process.

How much does pressure washing cost?

Pricing is based on square footage, level of organic growth (algae/moss), and surface type. Average house wash services run between $290 and $550. Premium concrete driveways usually run $200 to $450. We offer a transparent, line-item instant quote detailing everything before locking in schedule dates.

What is soft washing?

Soft washing is a low-pressure cleaning method (under 300 PSI, similar to a garden hose) that relies on professional-grade biodegradable cleaning agents to safely sanitize roofs, wood decks, and delicate vinyl siding. It kills organic spores at their roots instead of just blasting the top layer with high pressure, which can erode materials.

Is your process safe for pets and landscaping?

Absolutely. We practice thorough pre-wetting and post-rinsing of all grass, shrubs, and gardens around your home, diluted to safe levels. We use plant wash shields when necessary, and all sanitizers break down into simple table salts shortly after drying. We only request that pets remain inside while the crews work.

How soon can I get scheduled?

During spring and summer peak cycles, our schedules fill up 1 to 2 weeks out. We maintain backup slots for urgent sales transactions or emergency cleanings. Request a quote today to view the current live calendar and secure a time that works for you.

Are you fully licensed and insured?

Yes. Saddleback Wash Co. carries $2,000,000 in general commercial liability coverage and full workers' compensation coverage for our team. We protect you and your property. Verification certificates are emailed automatically with every work confirmation.
