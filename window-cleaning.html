Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding pure water filtration, screens, frames, and cleaning in the rain.

What is Pure Water window cleaning?

Pure Water cleaning uses water that has been filtered through carbon, reverse osmosis, and deionization systems to remove 100% of minerals. When sprayed on glass, it acts like a magnet for dirt and dries completely spot-free without squeegee tracks or chemical soap film.

Do you clean the inside of windows?

We specialize primarily in exterior window cleaning using pure-water fed poles, which provides the safest and most effective results for outdoor glass, tracks, and screens. For interior window detailing, please contact us for customized availability.

Will cleaning my windows in the rain make them dirty again?

No. Rainwater is pure and clean. Windows get dirty because rain hits existing dust, pollen, and chemical soap film on the glass. Since our pure water system leaves zero residue, rain will simply bead up and slide off, leaving the glass clean.
